Aim

Being a student is not easy and you may end up with missed opportunities, which could have benefited you in your career. While concentrating on certain things we may end up forgetting something which is more important, that may be the learning process, social interaction or yourself. For such situvations this website is the perfect solution, where students can know about upcoming events in their locality, and improve their skills by being a part of it.
